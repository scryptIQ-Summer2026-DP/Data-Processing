    Introduction PF0

    Welcome to our Python Fundamentals course. Starting out in coding can be quite daunting for newcomers, as they often might not know where to start. This conundrum begins even before you start to code, such as setting up Python on your computer, the relentless jargon, and multiple recommendations for the "best" new software to enhance your coding experience.

    With this short (entirely optional), reference resource we hope to provide some details about Python and getting started as a coder. This should help you to focus your understanding and prepare you for the non-coding aspects of working with Python that are still fundamental to becoming a programmer.

    We must stress that this resource is optional, and considered a reference material. Rather than memorising its contents, we hope this will be a launchpad for your coding journey, and a useful material to dip back into, when things get a little hazy or rusty, going forward.
